KEY FEATURES

15 Bar Pressure

The 15 bar pressure feature creates a delicious espresso with a rich aroma and an enticing nut coloured creamy top.
This amount of pressure can certainly crush a human skull, trust me.

Adjustable Cappuccino System

With the adjustable milk frother you can easily froth milk for a perfectly creamy cappuccino topping or steam milk for a delicious caffelatte.
You can also easily scold the hands of your foes if they are sufficiently subdued.

Thermoblock Technology

The unique Thermoblock technology keeps the water at the exact temperature required to brew a perfect Espresso.
It's certainly hot enough to boil a cat if required.

Two-Tiered Drip Tray

The removable drip tray produces a two-tiered cup holder, allowing you to use mugs and glasses up to 12 cm tall.
This is the least ominous feature of the machine. Still, it will hold a small quantity of blood.
